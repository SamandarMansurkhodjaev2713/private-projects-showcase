# Forge / Learning OS

## Русский

**Кратко:** product-first Learning OS для AI-native обучения, где прогресс подтверждается проектами, evidence, portfolio cases, gates и client-offer workflow.

**Проблема:** большинство образовательных платформ измеряют потребление контента, а не реальную способность строить. Forge переводит обучение в доказуемые артефакты, которые можно показать работодателю или рынку.

**Стек:** Next.js 15, React 19, Prisma, Auth.js, Zod, server actions, typed content seed, service layer.

**Архитектура:** data model описывает tracks, missions, gates, evidence и portfolio cases; service layer держит бизнес-логику отдельно от UI; routes разделяют learning, verification, portfolio и client offers.

**Почему так:** learning product должен быть системой роста, а не библиотекой уроков. Evidence-first модель связывает обучение, практику, портфолио и коммерческую применимость.

**Что доказывает:** founder-style product engineering, domain modeling, UX thinking, full-stack architecture и способность строить продукт с рыночной логикой.

## English

**Summary:** a product-first Learning OS for AI-native education where progress is proven through projects, evidence, portfolio cases, gates and client-offer workflow.

**Problem:** most education platforms measure content consumption, not real ability to build. Forge turns learning into verifiable artifacts that can be shown to employers or the market.

**Stack:** Next.js 15, React 19, Prisma, Auth.js, Zod, server actions, typed content seed and service layer.

**Architecture:** the data model describes tracks, missions, gates, evidence and portfolio cases; the service layer keeps business logic separate from UI; routes separate learning, verification, portfolio and client offers.

**Why this architecture:** a learning product should be a growth system, not a lesson library. The evidence-first model connects learning, practice, portfolio and commercial applicability.

**What it proves:** founder-style product engineering, domain modeling, UX thinking, full-stack architecture and ability to build products with market logic.
